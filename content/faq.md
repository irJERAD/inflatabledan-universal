## 1. How long does it take to get a quote?

We cannot gaurentee any verbal prices given without a visual inspection of the vehicle on which the damage or work to be done. However, we don't play around like most shops and wait until you waist precious time getting your boat out of the water and into the shop before slapping you with an insulting and completely unexpected quote.

Give us a call, or leave an email ~ Pictures are Great!_ ~ and we'll do our best to get to put together a reliably accurate price range of solutions and services.  

## 2. Do you only work on inflatable Boats or can you do fiberglass?

Many Inflatable boats actually have a fiber glass hoal. Of course we can help you patch up dings in your fiber glass hoal. Even dingie racers or small cruisers can be found coming into our workshop for small patchups such as reinstalling a box or runners a to a fresh coat of bottom paint.
